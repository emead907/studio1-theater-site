A PLACE TO GROW,
A STAGE TO BELONG.

Studio 1 Theater Project in Boise, Idaho is a creative space where kids, teens, and adults build confidence, teamwork, and performance skills through theater.

We focus on hands-on learning in a supportive environment, guiding students from auditions to live performance. Whether you are brand new to theater or already love the stage, Studio 1 helps performers grow on stage and beyond.

Students explore all parts of theater, including auditions, scripts, songs, choreography, stage craft, and set design.

THE FULL THEATER EXPERIENCE

Our 6-week theater session gives kids, teens, and adults the complete experience of putting on a live theatrical production.

Students attend rehearsals twice a week while learning acting, movement, stage presence, teamwork, choreography, and stage craft.

Everything builds toward a final live performance for family, friends, and the community.
