Privacy Policy

Studio 1 Theater Project respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you:

  • Fill out a contact form
  • Register for classes, camps, or productions
  • Subscribe to updates or newsletters
  • Communicate with us through email or online forms

How We Use Information

Information collected may be used to:

  • Process registrations and enrollments
  • Communicate important updates and scheduling information
  • Respond to questions or inquiries
  • Improve our programs and website experience

Third Party Services

Registration and enrollment forms may be processed through trusted third party services such as DreamClass. These services may collect and store information according to their own privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

Because our programs involve children, parent or guardian information may be collected during registration. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children without parent or guardian involvement.

Information Security

We take reasonable steps to help protect personal information submitted through our website and registration systems.

Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact Studio 1 Theater Project directly.
