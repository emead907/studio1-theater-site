About the 6-Week Session

Our 6-week theater session gives kids, teens, and adults the complete experience of putting on a live theatrical production.

Participants attend rehearsals twice a week while learning acting, movement, teamwork, stage presence, choreography, and stage craft.

Everything leads up to a final live performance where students shine on stage in front of family, friends, and the community.
